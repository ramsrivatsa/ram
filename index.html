Ram Srivatsa Kannan

I build large scale distributed systems at Uber! I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor under the supervision of Professor Jason Mars and Professor Lingjia Tang. Before that, I was an undergraduate student at Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University. During my undergrad days, I had the pleasure of working with Professor Venkateswaran Nagarajan.

What I Do

Computer Science

I am intersted in distributed systems, systems performance, computer architecture, operating systems and compilers. Specifically, I have worked on analyzing systems performance for enabling fairness in multi-tenant cloud computing environments.
